Product
Engineering

We turn concepts into reality from product design to rapid prototyping, all under one roof.

Rapid Prototyping

With focus on core rapid prototyping, we offer design and validation services creating superior-quality parts with an extensive selection of materials and production techniques, we make completely functional, high-quality prototypes at a competitive cost across industries.

Product Designing

We offer end-to-end new product design & development services, addressing the industrial needs in India and internationally. We specialise in Catia, Siemens NX, and Creo enabling us to deliver customised solutions tailored for initial vehicle design to series production. We manage concept design, modelling, and visualisation of products, boasting proficiency in bodywork, interior, exterior, and equipment design.

Manufacturing
Engineering

Expertise in BIW fixture engineering, controls engineering, die design, robotics simulation, and SPM.

BIW Fixture Design

With our expertise in BIW fixtures ranging from welding and joining, automated sub-assembly lines, final assembly lines, and material handling systems, we are a complete source of BIW solutions. We deliver designs with accuracy, repeatability, strength, modularity, and durability.

Controls Engineering

With over 13,000 manhours in design solutions, we specialise in electrical design and automation using top software like e-PLAN, AutoCAD, and E3 Zuken. Our USP is to deliver global-standard solutions, including schematics, 3D layouts, GA diagrams, and cable schedules, ensuring high-quality, efficient results for panel builders, and machine manufacturers, across various industries worldwide.

BIW Die Design

We deliver precision and innovation in 3D Automotive Stamping Die Design using CATIA V5 and Siemens NX, serving OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers worldwide. Our services include simulation, formability analysis, and die design for tandem and transfer press lines. We are world leaders in enhancing manufacturing efficiency and delivering high-quality, cost-effective solutions.

Robotics Simulation

We specialise in comprehensive digital manufacturing engineering solutions for production line automation, from process planning and layout to robotic simulation, offline programming, and safety assessments. Our expertise includes robotic cell design, task optimisation, safety assessments, and event-based simulation. With a focus on efficiency and precision, we deliver tailored automation solutions and offer global onsite manpower support for seamless project execution.

Special Purpose Machinery

We offer custom-made, fatigue-less, cost-effective SPM for specific client needs encompassing solutions that cover from automation to fulfilling mass production demands with desired quality and quantity. We have championed general purpose & welding purpose SPM, automotive and non-automotive assembly line machine tools & fixtures, assembly robo cells, fabricated structures, 3D & 2D models, reverse engineering and support.
