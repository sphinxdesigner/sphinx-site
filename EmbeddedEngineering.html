Hardware Design and Development-

We specialise in end-to-end electronics solutions and support, including circuit design, simulation, PCB fabrication, and assembly. Our team excels in embedded software development, debugging, and testing using advanced tools like MATLAB, Proteus, Tessy, and EasyEDA. Our expertise includes schematic design, breakout board realisation, and precise Gerber file generation for seamless manufacturing. With expertise in platforms such as STM32, Raspberry Pi, and ESP32, we create robust IoT, industrial, and automotive systems that meet stringent performance, reliability, and compliance standards.

FreeRTOS Integration

We integrate FreeRTOS into embedded systems, optimise task management, do real-time scheduling, and optimise resource utilisation. Next is ensuring seamless integration with platforms like STM32, ESP32, and NXP microcontrollers, enforcing efficient multitasking and low-latency performance for IoT, industrial, and automotive applications. We leverage our experience with FreeRTOS's APIs and advanced debugging tools to deliver reliable and scalable solutions that meet the demands of modern embedded systems.

Firmware Development

We focus on creating reliable, efficient, and hardware-optimised code for embedded systems. We develop firmware customised for specific hardware using languages like C, C++, and Python, and tools such as MATLAB, Simulink, and GCC. Our expertise encompasses advanced debugging, static code analysis, and Hardware-in-the-Loop (HIL) testing, ensuring robust performance, seamless device communication, and compliance with industry standards for automotive, IoT, and industrial applications.

Testing & Validation

Industry & Applications of Embedded Solutions

Embedded services are integrated functionalities that operate as part of a larger system, designed to provide seamless, real-time operations, and enhance overall system performance.

Unlike standalone tools or applications, these services work in harmony with their host platform, offering tailored solutions that meet specific needs across diverse industries. Their adaptability and efficiency have made them indispensable in modern engineering systems.

At Sphinx Worldbiz, we honour and meet the demands of a rapidly evolving technological landscape with our customised embedded engineering systems.

Automotive: Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS), EV System Integration, V2X Communication

We develop advanced autonomous vehicle features and manufacturers can improve their safety, efficiency, and driving experience using features like adaptive cruise control, real-time GPS navigation, collision detection systems, and vehicle-to-everything (V2X) communication.

Healthcare: RT-PCR System Development, Medical Firmware Development

Using embedded engineering, we develop advanced RT-PCR (Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction) systems crucial for rapid diagnostic tests. These embedded solutions are integrated into medical devices or lab systems, allowing real-time detection of pathogens and other genetic material with enhanced speed, accuracy, and reliability, significantly benefiting patient outcomes and public health initiatives.

Consumer IoT: Smart Devices and Home Automation Solutions

Our IoT-enabled smart devices with embedded sensors and monitoring tools that collect and analyse data in real-time, enable applications like home automation, energy management, and industrial monitoring.

Industrial Automation: Embedded Sensors, Control Systems

We leverage advanced embedded sensors and control systems while embedding industrial automation solutions to enhance precision, efficiency, and reliability across industrial processes. Our complete solution entails design and integration with predictive maintenance and process optimisation. Paired with robust control systems, our solutions deliver seamless automation, improved operational accuracy, and reduced downtime.

FAQ

Embedded engineering involves designing and developing software and hardware systems that are integrated into a device or product to perform specific functions. From microcontrollers to complex embedded systems, this technology powers everything from consumer electronics to industrial equipment. By leveraging embedded engineering, your company can create highly efficient, reliable, and innovative products that meet specific market needs.

Embedded engineering services are vital for a variety of industries, including:

Consumer Electronics: Smart devices, wearables, and IoT products.
Automotive: Infotainment systems, safety systems, and autonomous driving technologies.
Healthcare: Medical devices, monitoring systems, and diagnostic tools.
Industrial Automation: Robotics, machinery, and factory control systems.
Aerospace & Defence: Flight control systems, communication devices, and surveillance equipment.
Agriculture: Precision farming tools, automation, and monitoring systems.

We develop a wide range of embedded systems, including:

Microcontroller-based systems for basic applications.
Real-time operating systems (RTOS) for time-sensitive applications.
IoT devices for smart, connected products.
Battery-powered systems for low-energy applications.
High-performance embedded systems for complex processing and control.

Whatever your project requires, we can tailor the embedded system to fit your product's needs.

Embedded systems are highly optimized for specific tasks, meaning they can perform better while using fewer resources. By designing embedded solutions that focus on power efficiency, real-time processing, and low overhead, you can lower manufacturing costs, reduce power consumption, and extend the lifespan of your products, ultimately leading to a higher ROI.

Key advantages of embedded engineering:

Optimized Performance: Embedded systems are tailored to the specific needs of your product, offering faster execution and greater reliability.
Lower Power Consumption: Embedded systems are designed to operate efficiently, reducing energy usage and extending battery life.
Cost-Efficiency: With a focused design, embedded systems can help lower both manufacturing and operational costs.
Customization: Embedded systems can be fully customized to meet the exact needs of your product, ensuring it performs as expected.
