Our Services

BIW Die Design is crucial in automotive manufacturing, demanding materials, mechanics, and advanced software expertise. A well-crafted die enhances product quality, consistency, and manufacturing efficiency, thus, reducing costs. We offer competent solutions for BIW Die Designing which comprises of the following processes.

Simulation & Formability analysis

We deliver precision in design through advanced tools and expert formability analysis, identifying and resolving concerns early to ensure efficiency. We excel in sheet metal expertise, digital prototyping, and multi-stage forming simulations. Our services, including 3D CAD modelling, material optimisation, and defect prediction, reduce costs and enhance production, ensuring high accuracy and seamless collaboration with our clients globally.

3D Process/Method Plan

At this stage, we define the correct process and mould design based on feasibility studies, focusing on material behaviour and precise manufacturing planning. We ensure high-quality using advanced tools, cost-effective components, and optimising methods to enhance capabilities and achieve superior results in producing complex parts.

3D BIW Die Design

In 3D BIW Die Design, our services include progressive, bending, transfer, and prototype dies. As a leader in die design services provider in India, we serve Tier 1 suppliers globally, optimising performance, reducing costs, and ensuring superior quality while adhering to stringent standards.

Cam & Press Simulation

Using Computer-Aided Manufacturing (CAM) digital die designs are translated into machine instructions for precise fabrication of tooling and patterns. Press simulation helps in modelling processes, including material flow and press kinematics, with tools like Autoform and full-cycle simulation. Together, they enhance production quality, reduce costs, and ensure reliability by pre-empting potential defects before production.

2D Technical Drawing

Often considered the foundation for creating 3D models, 2D technical drawings represent the detailed dimensions and specifications of dies and tools conveying the correct and precise design intent to the designers, engineers, and manufacturers. We prepare 2D designs and they facilitate the 3D designs followed by the CAM processes and simulations before manufacturing and documentation.

Investing in custom BIW Die Design offers several key benefits:

• Precision and Accuracy: Custom-designed dies ensure that parts are formed accurately with minimal defects, improving overall product quality.
• Cost Savings: High-quality BIW Die Designs reduce material waste, lower the need for rework, and streamline production, leading to cost savings.
• Faster Production: A well-designed die allows for faster cycle times and efficient mass production, helping meet tight deadlines.
• Longer Lifespan: Durable dies reduce maintenance costs and ensure long-term reliability, making your investment more cost-effective in the long run.

Our BIW Die Design process typically involves the following stages:

1. Initial Consultation & Needs Assessment: We collaborate with you to understand the product requirements, production volume, and material characteristics.
2. Conceptualisation: Our team develops initial design concepts based on your specifications and production goals.
3. CAD Modelling: We create detailed 3D CAD models to visualise and simulate the die’s functionality before fabrication.
4. Prototyping & Testing: We build prototypes and test them to ensure they meet quality standards and production requirements.
5. Final Design & Production: After testing, we refine the design and prepare the die for manufacturing, ensuring everything is optimised for production.
