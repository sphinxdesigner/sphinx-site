COMPANY OVERVIEW

Incorporated in 1996 and based in India, Sphinx Worldbiz has steadily risen over the past 29 years to become a dynamic leader in a vivacious all things engineering! With a strong global presence, the company has grown into an engineering mammoth, offering comprehensive engineering solutions and driving innovation across industries, making its mark as a trusted name in the advancing world.

With 'Engineering-first' as its motto, Sphinx Worldbiz has grown to the size of an 800+ workforce of exceptional and qualified talent pool with expertise in end-to-end engineering design solutions, product designing, scalable staffing, and integration with software solutions.

With a truly global presence, Sphinx Worldbiz is also present in the heart of Europe- Germany, Sweden along with the United Kingdom, the Americas, and Asia.
