The Purpose of BIW Fixture Design for Automotives

We specialise in designing high-precision BIW (Body-in-White) fixtures tailored to the automotive industry's exacting standards. BIW fixtures are critical tools used to align and position automotive BIW parts during welding and assembly processes. These fixtures ensure seamless integration of components, enabling precise fits and optimised production efficiency. With our expertise, we deliver innovative solutions that enhance accuracy, reduce lead times, and drive superior manufacturing outcomes.

Fixtures improve the manufacturing process by:

• Increasing Precision: Custom fixtures securely hold components in the exact position needed, ensuring accurate machining or assembly.
• Reducing Downtime: Efficient fixtures speed up setup times, enabling faster production cycles.
• Minimising Errors: Fixtures eliminate human error by automating repetitive tasks and ensuring consistent results.
• Optimising Production Flow: By improving handling, automation, and alignment, fixtures streamline the overall process, helping to reduce waste and increase throughput.

Fixture design is valuable across many industries, including:

• Automotive: For assembly lines, welding, and quality control.
• Aerospace: For complex parts that require high precision.
• Electronics: For testing, assembly, and PCB handling.
• Medical Devices: For precise handling of delicate components.
• Industrial Manufacturing: For machining, assembly, and automation processes.
• Consumer Goods: For assembly and packaging processes.

Any business that requires precise, repeatable manufacturing can benefit from customised fixture designs.

We design a wide range of fixtures, including:

• Machining Fixtures: For holding parts during CNC milling, turning, and grinding operations.
• Assembly Fixtures: To position and hold parts during assembly processes, ensuring correct alignment and ease of handling.
• Welding Fixtures: For securely holding components during welding to maintain alignment and prevent distortion.
• Inspection Fixtures: For quality control and testing purposes, ensuring parts meet specification.
• Custom Fixtures: Tailored to your unique production needs or specialised applications.

Whatever your manufacturing or assembly requirements, we can create a fixture that meets your needs.
